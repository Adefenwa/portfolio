FarmConnect Uganda — Final Project Report

Report Writing

I co-wrote the final project report for FarmConnect Uganda — a platform connecting smallholder farmers to urban markets. The report documented community growth metrics showing 366% growth in registered farmers, real user testimonials, stakeholder feedback from cooperatives and government offices, and a full summary of achievements and recommended next steps. A complete, professional document for a real live project.

Mobile Payments & Regulations — Research Document

Research & Analysis

I researched mobile payment options in Uganda for the FarmConnect project — comparing MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money and bank apps. I put together a structured document covering key regulations, transaction limits, a risk assessment table and strategic recommendations. This required reading official sources, comparing multiple options and presenting findings clearly so the team could act on them.

Online Research Structured Documentation Risk Assessment Recommendations Writing Comparative Analysis

Partnership Agreements — 10 Organisations

Outreach & Documentation

I established and documented 10 partnership agreements with farming associations and cooperatives across Nigeria — from Lagos, Ibadan and Oyo State to Kano, Kaduna and Enugu. Each entry recorded the contact person, agreement type, date signed and specific notes on the scope. This documentation directly supported FarmConnect's pre-launch outreach strategy across multiple states.
